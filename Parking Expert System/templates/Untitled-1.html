Artificial Intelligence in Web Development

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming the landscape of web development by enabling smarter, more dynamic websites and applications. With AI, developers can build web platforms that learn from user behavior, improve performance, and provide personalized experiences.

Key applications of AI in web development include:

  • Chatbots for customer service
  • Personalized content and recommendations
  • Voice search optimization
  • Automated testing and debugging
  • Enhanced user experience with predictive analytics
